Hey. I’m Cameron. I’m a network admin for a small cellular company in NZ. I like hiking, music production, video games and the free and open-source software movements. I like other stuff, but I forgot tbh. I’ll tell you later if I remember.

This is a blog I felt compelled to create as I dive further into open-source. I keep a lot of scattered notes on my own setups that benefit me when I need them. Most of the time, they’re just quirks of my installs or information that’s already been done better in other guides online, but sometimes I come across something unusual that’s worth a document. If I remember to put it here, it might be of use to others in the future. Fingers crossed!
